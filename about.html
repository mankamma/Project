Welcome to HIC, your passport to a world of culinary delight. Nestled in the heart of Toronto, our dim sum restaurant is a gateway to the exquisite world of bite-sized Chinese wonders.


At HIC, we celebrate the art of dim sum, offering a diverse selection of steamed, fried, and baked delicacies that are perfect for sharing with friends and family. With an ambiance that combines modern comfort and traditional charm, our restaurant provides the ideal setting for a memorable dining experience.


Our skilled chefs craft each dim sum dish with precision, using the freshest ingredients and time -honored techniques. From classic dumplings to innovative creations, our menu promises an authentic journey through the flavors of Chinese cuisine.
